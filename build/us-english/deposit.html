deposit

Phonetic: /dɪˈpɒzɪt/

Part of Speech: noun

Category: financial term

Definition: In most web3 contexts, 'depositing' refers to the act of transferring some amount of token(s) to an address other than one's own, most often to a contract.html">smart contract controlled by a 'protocol', such as a exchange.html">decentralized exchange, video game or multiverse, DAO, etc. Generally, the user will receive something in return for their deposit, and the deposit can be claimed at the user's discretion, or upon completion of given conditions. Compare with 'stake'.
