NFT drop

Phonetic: /ɛn ɛf ˈtiː drɒp/

Part of Speech: noun

Category: decentralized web

Definition: An NFT drop happens when a new NFT collection is released. NFT drops can vary in both how the NFTs are sold (listed for sale or auction), and in who they’re released to (the public, or a specific list called an “allowlist”).. , NFT drops coincide with when the NFTs in the collection are minted, that is, written to the blockchain. You might hear these terms used interchangeably— a drop might be referred to as the project’s mint.
