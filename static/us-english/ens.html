ENS

Phonetic: /ɪˈθiːriəm neɪm ˈsɜrvɪs/, /ˈɛns/

Part of Speech: noun

Category:

Definition: Initialism; the Ethereum Name Service is a protocol, managed by a DAO, which assigns human-readable and easy-to-remember addresses to Ethereum addresses and assets, homologous to the traditional internet’s DNS.
