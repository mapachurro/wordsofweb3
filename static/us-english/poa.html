PoA

Phonetic: /ˈpruːf əv ɔˈθɒrɪti/, /ˌpiː oʊ ˈeɪ/

Part of Speech: noun

Category:

Definition: Initialism; Proof of Authority. A consensus mechanism used in private blockchains, granting a single private key the authority to generate all of the blocks or validate transactions.
