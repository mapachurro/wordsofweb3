block reward

Phonetic: /blɑk rɪˈwɔrd/

Part of Speech: noun

Category: decentralized web

Definition: The reward given to a miner or validator after it has successfully hashed a block.html">transaction block. Block rewards can be a mixture of coins and transaction fees. The composition depends on the policy used by the cryptocurrency in question, and whether all of the coins have already been successfully mined. The current block reward for the Bitcoin network is 12.5 bitcoins per block.
